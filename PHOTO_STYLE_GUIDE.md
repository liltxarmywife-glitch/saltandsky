# Salt & Sky Photography Style Guide

## The Core Rule
**Every image should make someone think: "I wish that were my memory."**

If an image could be mistaken for a stock travel advertisement, we don't use it.

## The Test
- Faces toward each other, not the camera ✓
- Someone captured the moment; nobody posed for it ✓
- It feels like eavesdropping on someone's favorite memory ✓

## What We Don't Use
- Everyone smiling at the camera in perfect composition → stock photo
- Cocktails held toward the lens → Instagram influencer, not Salt & Sky
- Too polished, too centered, too "smile for Grandma"
- Anything that looks like it came from a travel brochure

---

## Image Categories

### 1. Families
Grandparents. Parents. Children. Multiple generations.
- Nobody looking at the camera
- Walking on the beach, building sandcastles, watching kids play in the water
- Grandparent holding a grandchild's hand
- Parents watching children run into the ocean
- Evening — golden hour light, everyone gathered

### 2. Couples
Holding hands. Coffee on a balcony. Walking. Watching sunsets together.
- Not kissing every photo — just existing together
- Sitting on a porch looking out, not at each other's faces at the camera
- Senior couple laughing at something we can't hear (#10 energy)
- Anniversary dinners, slow mornings, quiet beach walks

### 3. Friends
Laughing. Dinner table. Fire pit. Exploring a market or street.
- Toasting without looking at the camera (#3 energy exactly)
- Mid-laugh, mid-conversation
- Walking ahead of the frame, backs to camera
- Not posed group shots — candid clusters

### 4. Celebrations
Proposal. Graduation. Anniversary. Birthday. Family reunion.
- The moment of reaction, not the setup
- Graduation cap on a beach at sunset (#12 energy)
- Someone being surprised
- Family reunion — generations spread across a lawn or beach

### 5. Quiet Moments (Section Dividers)
Solo or small. These slow the page down.
- Coffee cup on a railing, ocean in the background
- Person reading in a hammock, face obscured
- Feet in the sand, looking at the water
- Sunrise over water — no people required
- Child's hand in an adult's hand
- Empty breakfast table on a balcony with a view

## ChatGPT Prompt Template
Use this structure for every new image request:

> Photorealistic travel lifestyle photo. [Scene description]. Nobody is looking at the camera — they are [interacting with each other / watching the view / laughing together]. Golden hour light. Warm, soft, cinematic. Feels like a candid memory, not a staged advertisement. High resolution, horizontal format.
